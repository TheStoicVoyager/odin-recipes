How to Make Toast

A refined classic in the world of bread lovers

Ingredients

  • Slice of Bread
  • Condiments (optional):
    • butter
    • jam

Instructions (may be complicated for novice chefs)

  1. Acquire a toaster and plug it in
  2. Put your slice of bread in any slot
  3. Set your desired toastiness setting (will improve with practice)
  4. Push the start button and go find your condiments
  5. Once the bread has elevated place it on a plate
  6. Spread your condiments if desired and viola you have toast
