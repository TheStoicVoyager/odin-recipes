How to make a Bagel

A soft circular piece of bread similar to the doughnut

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Condiments (optional):
    • Peanut Butter
    • Jam
    • Sour Cream
    • Slice of Cheese

Instructions (slighter more complicated than a slice of bread)

  1. Acquire a toaster and plug it in
  2. Put your bagel in any slot
  3. Set your desired toastiness setting (will improve with practice)
  4. Push the start button and go find your condiments
  5. Once the bread has elevated place it on a plate
  6. Spread your condiments if desired and viola you have a bagel
