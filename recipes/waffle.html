How to Make a Waffle

More complex piece of bread with geometric shapes carved within

Ingredients

  • Freezer Waffle
  • Condiments (optional):
    • butter
    • syrup
    • peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Acquire a toaster and plug it in
  2. Put your waffle in any slot
  3. Set your desired toastiness setting (will improve with practice)
  4. Push the start button and go find your condiments
  5. Once the bread has elevated place it on a plate
  6. Spread your condiments if desired and viola you have a waffle
