How to Make a Bagel

Bagels are fine pieces of bread and make easy breakfast meals

Ingredients:

  • Bagel
  • Toaster
  • Preferred Condiment

Instructions:

  1. Grab your favorite type of Bagel
  2. Grab your favorite brand of toaster
  3. Plug in the toaster and put the Bagel in
  4. Set your ideal toastyness setting and push start
  5. Once it pops out, put your ideal condiment upon the Bagel
  6. BAM, your Bagel is done
